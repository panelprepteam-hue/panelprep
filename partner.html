What It Means to Be a PanelPrep Partner

As a Partner, you will:

  • Conduct structured mock interviews in your domain.
  • Maintain professional standards.
  • Provide realistic and constructive evaluation.
  • Help candidates understand not just what to answer, but how to think.

You will operate within a standardized framework that ensures quality and consistency.

Partner Benefits

As a PanelPrep Partner, you will:

  • Monetize your interview experience in a structured way.
  • Strengthen your professional brand and credibility.
  • Develop leadership and mentoring skills.
  • Expand your network across high-performing professionals.
  • Work with flexible scheduling based on your availability.
  • Receive transparent and structured payouts.
